Zale

Solar Bladedancer

Born on the Summer Solstice, Zale is optimistic and bold. Like all Solstice Warriors, his innate magic allows him to create "Live Mana" when attacking, which his party can use to boost their attacks

The Elder Mist foretold that, in order to find his light and achieve his full potential, he will first need to stare at the night inside of him.

Weapon : Sunblade
