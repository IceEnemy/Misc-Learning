Garl

Warrior Cook

Garl was born a regular kid devoid of any special powers. Along with his training as a cook, his innately strong constitution make him a capable defensive fighter, giving him the tools to support his two best friends in their Solstice Warrior duties.

The Elder Mist saw something peculiar in the stars about him, as it seems his heart just might be warm enough to soothe a long tormented soul.
